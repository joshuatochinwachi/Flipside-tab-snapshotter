Introduction
This dashboard delivers a comprehensive behavioral analysis of both DEX Traders and Liquidity Providers within the Avalanche ecosystem. Using the Flipside Intelligence Driven Growth (IDG) methodology, I employed a structured, data-driven approach to unpack user journeys, engagement patterns, platform usage, and liquidity contribution across key DEX platforms on Avalanche over the past 12 months (May 2024 ‚Äì April 2025).

User Segmentation Framework

Analytical Value & Insights
This dashboard offers actionable insights for growth teams, DEX operators, ecosystem foundations, and data analysts by highlighting:

  • Engagement & Behavior: Tracks volume, action frequency, retention, and cohort trends across user tiers

  • Liquidity Dynamics: Measures LP-specific metrics including pool participation, average volume per LP, and DEX-specific behaviors

  • Retention & Churn: Quantifies how well users and LPs are retained over time, including cohort retention and reactivation success

  • Growth Opportunities: Identifies underperforming tiers and provides tier-specific incentive strategies to drive activation and retention

Strategic Insights
This analysis reveals that top-tier users (Whales/Sharks), while fewer, drive the majority of volume and exhibit strong retention. In contrast, low-tier users (Crab/Shrimp) comprise the bulk of the user base but contribute minimally to volume and show higher churn‚Äîindicating areas ripe for reactivation and engagement strategies.

Analyzing user and LP behavior side-by-side further exposes overlapping trends and divergence in contribution, enabling Avalanche ecosystem teams to tailor interventions with precision.

This structured approach provides the Avalanche ecosystem with a solid foundation for:
